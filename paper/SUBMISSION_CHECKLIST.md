# URTC Submission Checklist

Complete these confirmations only after personally reviewing the final paper
and the submission rules for the current conference year.

- [ ] Every listed author satisfies the current URTC student eligibility rules.
- [ ] The paper is original, author-owned work and all required permissions are secured.
- [ ] The paper is not published, accepted, or under review elsewhere in substantially similar form.
- [ ] The current URTC track, template, deadline, and submission instructions have been confirmed.
- [ ] The final title, author names, affiliations, and contact details are accurate.
- [ ] Every citation and bibliography entry has been personally verified against its source.
- [ ] Every quantitative claim is traceable to a repository result or clearly identified methodology.
- [ ] Limitations distinguish simulation proxies from measured hardware results and full formal proof.
- [ ] The final PDF has been read end to end after the last build and contains no private information.
- [ ] At least one eligible listed author can register and present the paper if accepted.

Current submission guidance: https://urtc.mit.edu/submission
